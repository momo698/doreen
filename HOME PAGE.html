MY COLLEGE COICT.

The College of Information and Communication Technologies ( CoICT ) is among the core academic units of the University of Dar es salaam ( UDSM ) main campus. CoICT was established by the council of the University of Dar es salaam on January 27th 2011 by merging the former School of Information and Communication Technologies ( SICT ) with the Department of Electrical and Computer System Engineering ( ECSE ) which was under the College of Engineering and Technology ( CoET ) .
